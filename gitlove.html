Dear Git,
We initiate our conversations without any argument. We have only spent a few weeks together, but I know this relationship will last. I can always check the git status of our relationship and without any hesitation you will tell me exactly where we are. Adding to our relationship is effortless and you somehow always remember what I said when I commit a message to you. I love your adventurous side and how you are willing to checkout a different branch with me. If we love a branch, it is never an issue to merge this new branch with our current branch by using git merge.

I can't wait to keep the adventure going with you.
Love,
Erin
